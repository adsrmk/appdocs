# Welcome to Heurs for employers

Learn how to manage employees, time, leave and organization settings in one place.

<p>Heurs gives employers one central workspace for employees, time registrations, leave and organization settings.</p>
<h2>The main areas</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Employees:</strong> invite team members and monitor their current status.</li>
<li><strong>Time registrations:</strong> review submitted weeks and follow up on missing entries.</li>
<li><strong>Leave:</strong> assess requests with visibility into team availability.</li>
<li><strong>Settings:</strong> define the workweek, staffing target, leave policy and notifications.</li>
</ul>
<div class="callout callout-info"><strong>Keep the employee experience simple</strong><p>Employees register their hours and request leave. You configure the organization, monitor progress and process items that need approval.</p>
</div>
<h2>Recommended setup order</h2>
<ol>
<li>Verify your organization details.</li>
<li>Configure the standard workweek and leave policy.</li>
<li>Invite employees.</li>
<li>Review the dashboard when the first registrations arrive.</li>
</ol>
<h2>Daily workflow</h2>
<p>Start with the employee overview, then check notifications, time registrations and leave requests. Items without an outstanding action do not require a decision.</p>
