# Configure the leave policy

Manage accrual, balances and organization rules for leave.

<p>A clear leave policy keeps employee balances and approval decisions consistent.</p>
<h2>Define the policy</h2>
<p>Record how leave is granted or accrued, which effective date applies and how existing balances should be handled. Align the configuration with employment agreements and applicable rules.</p>
<h2>Verify employee balances</h2>
<p>After saving, review a small number of employee profiles. Confirm that balances and future accrual match your intended policy before using them for decisions.</p>
<h2>Change an existing policy</h2>
<p>Choose an appropriate effective date and document the reason for the change. Avoid silently rewriting historical decisions or balances.</p>
<div class="callout callout-info"><strong>Administrative guidance</strong><p>Heurs applies the settings you configure; it does not replace legal or payroll advice.</p>
