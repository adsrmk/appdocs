# Secure your employer account

Protect your management account with two-step verification.

<p>Employer accounts can access sensitive workforce information. Use a unique password and enable two-step verification.</p>
<h2>Enable two-step verification</h2>
<ol>
<li>Open your account or security settings.</li>
<li>Start the two-step verification setup.</li>
<li>Scan the QR code with an authenticator app.</li>
<li>Enter the generated code to confirm setup.</li>
<li>Store recovery information in a secure location.</li>
</ol>
<h2>Keep access secure</h2>
<ul>
<li>Never share your password or verification codes.</li>
<li>Remove access for people who no longer manage the organization.</li>
<li>Review unexpected login or account-change emails immediately.</li>
<li>Contact support when you lose access rather than creating a second account.</li>
</ul>
