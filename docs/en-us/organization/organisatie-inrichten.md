# Set up your organization

Configure the core details and defaults for your organization.

<p>Correct organization settings ensure that time, leave and availability calculations are reliable from the start.</p>
<h2>Check the basics</h2>
<p>Open <strong>Settings</strong> and verify the organization name and the details used throughout your workspace.</p>
<h2>Recommended order</h2>
<ol>
<li>Select the days that count as working days.</li>
<li>Set the target for team availability.</li>
<li>Review the leave policy and its effective date.</li>
<li>Choose the time reminders and email summaries your team needs.</li>
</ol>
<div class="callout callout-info"><strong>Configure first, invite second</strong><p>When you finish the organization settings before inviting employees, new team members immediately see the correct workweek and leave rules.</p>
</div>
<h2>Verify the result</h2>
<p>Open the employee and leave overviews. Confirm that working days, availability signals and calculated values look correct before communicating the policy to your team.</p>
