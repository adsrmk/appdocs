# Configure time reminders and notifications

Choose when your team receives reminders and summaries.

<p>Reminders help employees submit hours on time and help managers keep outstanding work visible.</p>
<h2>Choose useful reminders</h2>
<p>Enable reminders that match the submission rhythm of your organization. A weekly reminder is usually clearer than several messages about the same period.</p>
<h2>Configure manager summaries</h2>
<p>Use summaries to highlight missing registrations and requests waiting for approval. Keep the recipient list limited to people who can act on the information.</p>
<h2>Prevent notification fatigue</h2>
<p>Review reminder settings after the first few weeks. Disable duplicates and adjust timing when messages arrive too early or after the team has already completed the task.</p>
