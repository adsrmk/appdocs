# Frequently asked questions for employers

Quick answers about organization management, employees, time and leave.

<h2>Why is an employee still marked as invited?</h2>
<p>The employee has not completed account activation. Verify the email address and ask them to check spam. Create a new invitation when the previous link has expired.</p>
<h2>Why are hours shown as missing?</h2>
<p>The selected week is incomplete or has not been submitted. Also check whether the employee workweek and approved leave are configured correctly.</p>
<h2>What happens after I approve a time registration?</h2>
<p>The week is marked as processed and is included in the relevant organization overviews. Return the registration instead when a correction is required.</p>
<h2>Why does a leave request affect availability?</h2>
<p>Heurs compares planned absence with the configured working days and staffing target. The signal helps you assess coverage before deciding.</p>
<h2>Can I change organization settings later?</h2>
<p>Yes. Review the effective date and possible impact on future calculations before saving a policy change.</p>
<h2>I cannot find the answer</h2>
<p>Contact <a href="mailto:support@heurs.nl">support@heurs.nl</a> and include the page, period and employee concerned. Do not send passwords or verification codes.</p>
