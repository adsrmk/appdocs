# Follow up on missing hours

Identify incomplete weeks and remind employees to submit their hours.

<p>Use the time overview to find weeks that are incomplete or have not been submitted.</p>
<h2>Identify missing registrations</h2>
<p>Select the relevant week and filter for outstanding or incomplete entries. Compare registered hours with the employee’s configured workweek and approved leave.</p>
<h2>Send a useful reminder</h2>
<p>Ask the employee to complete and submit the specific week. Mention the period that is missing so the request is immediately actionable.</p>
<h2>Follow up</h2>
<p>After the employee submits the week, review it using the normal approval process. If hours remain missing, verify the workweek and leave settings before sending another reminder.</p>
