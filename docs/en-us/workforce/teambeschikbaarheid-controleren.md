# Check team availability

Review planned absence and prevent understaffing.

<p>Team availability combines working days and approved leave to show how much capacity remains.</p>
<h2>Choose the right period</h2>
<p>Open the leave or team overview and select the period covered by the request. Compare planned absence across employees instead of reviewing a request in isolation.</p>
<h2>Interpret the signal</h2>
<p>Use the target availability from your organization settings as a warning threshold. A low value does not automatically require rejection, but it signals that you should review coverage and priorities.</p>
<h2>Resolve conflicts</h2>
<p>Discuss alternatives with the employee when too many team members are absent at the same time. Record the final decision in Heurs so balances and overviews stay consistent.</p>
