# Use the employer dashboard

Understand the main figures and outstanding actions for your organization.

<p>The dashboard combines the current status of your team so you can quickly see where attention is needed.</p>
<h2>Understand the key figures</h2>
<div class="table-responsive"><table class="table table-vcenter">
<thead>
<tr>
<th>Figure</th>
<th>Meaning</th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td>Total hours worked</td>
<td>All registered hours for the selected week.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Average per employee</td>
<td>The average registered effort per team member.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Attendance</td>
<td>The attendance percentage based on organization settings.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Leave this week</td>
<td>Leave hours that fall within the selected week.</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table></div>
<h2>Use weeks and filters</h2>
<p>Select the correct week first. Then filter by status or role to review a smaller group. Clear the filters to return to the full overview.</p>
<h2>Follow up on actions</h2>
<p>Notification counters show whether leave requests are waiting for review. The navigation also shows outstanding time registrations. Open the corresponding page to inspect the details.</p>
