# Approve time registrations

Review submitted weeks and process employee hours.

<p>Time registrations are grouped by employee and week so you can review a complete period before making a decision.</p>
<h2>Review a submitted week</h2>
<ol>
<li>Open <strong>Time registrations</strong>.</li>
<li>Select a week and employee.</li>
<li>Compare registered hours with the expected workweek.</li>
<li>Review notes, leave and unusual differences.</li>
<li>Approve the week or return it for correction.</li>
</ol>
<h2>Before approval</h2>
<p>Check that working days are complete, leave is recorded consistently and corrections have been explained. Approval confirms that the submitted week is ready for further processing.</p>
<div class="callout callout-warning"><strong>Return incorrect entries</strong><p>Do not edit an unexplained registration on behalf of an employee. Return the week with a clear correction request so the audit trail remains understandable.</p>
</div>
