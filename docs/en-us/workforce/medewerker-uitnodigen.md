# Invite an employee

Add an employee with a personal email invitation.

<p>Add new team members from the employee overview.</p>
<h2>Before you start</h2>
<p>Use the business email address the employee will use for Heurs. Check the spelling carefully because the invitation and future profile are linked to this address.</p>
<h2>Send an invitation</h2>
<ol>
<li>Open <strong>Employees</strong>.</li>
<li>Select <strong>Invite employee</strong>.</li>
<li>Enter the employee name and email address.</li>
<li>Create the invitation and verify its delivery status.</li>
</ol>
<h2>After sending</h2>
<p>The employee receives an activation email. Until activation, the employee appears with the status <strong>Invited</strong>. If the link expires, create a new invitation; the previous activation link will become invalid.</p>
<div class="callout callout-warning"><strong>No email received?</strong><p>Check the address and ask the employee to inspect their spam folder before creating a new invitation.</p>
</div>
