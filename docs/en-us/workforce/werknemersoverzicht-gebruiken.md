# Use the employee overview

Review employees, invitations and relevant team information.

<p>The employee overview is the central starting point for team management.</p>
<h2>Understand statuses</h2>
<div class="table-responsive"><table class="table table-vcenter">
<thead>
<tr>
<th>Status</th>
<th>Meaning</th>
<th>Possible action</th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td>Active</td>
<td>The profile has been activated.</td>
<td>Review time, leave or employee details.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Invited</td>
<td>The invitation has not been accepted yet.</td>
<td>Wait or verify the email address.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Expired</td>
<td>The activation link is no longer valid.</td>
<td>Create a new invitation.</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table></div>
<h2>Refine the list</h2>
<p>Search by name or email. Use status and role filters to find open invitations or employees within a specific role.</p>
<h2>Open employee details</h2>
<p>Select an employee to review or update profile details. Verify contract hours and the hourly rate carefully; registered hours above contract hours are shown as overtime.</p>
