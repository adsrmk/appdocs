# View team insights and staffing

Review attendance, leave and employee effort by week.

<p>The team overview helps you monitor staffing and effort without opening every registration separately.</p>
<h2>Use the weekly view</h2>
<p>Choose a week to compare team members within the same period. Look for missing hours, planned leave and unusual attendance values.</p>
<h2>Read the overview</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Hours worked:</strong> the registered total for the selected week.</li>
<li><strong>Contract:</strong> the agreed weekly hours and recorded hourly rate.</li>
<li><strong>Last working day:</strong> the most recent day with a time entry.</li>
<li><strong>Status:</strong> active, invited or expired.</li>
<li><strong>Leave balance:</strong> the current balance recorded for the employee.</li>
</ul>
<h2>Search, filter and export</h2>
<p>Search by name or email, filter by status or role and sort by hours worked. Open an employee to review profile details, contract hours and calculated overtime.</p>
