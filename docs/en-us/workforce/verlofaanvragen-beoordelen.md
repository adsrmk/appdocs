# Review leave requests

Check requests and record your decision.

<p>Review the dates, requested hours, leave balance and impact on the team before approving or rejecting a request.</p>
<h2>Review a request</h2>
<ol>
<li>Open <strong>Leave</strong> and select a pending request.</li>
<li>Check the employee, dates and requested number of hours.</li>
<li>Review overlapping leave and expected team availability.</li>
<li>Approve or reject the request.</li>
<li>Add a clear explanation when a request is rejected.</li>
</ol>
<h2>After your decision</h2>
<p>Approved leave is included in availability and time overviews. Rejected leave does not change the balance. Recheck the team view when the request covers a busy period.</p>
