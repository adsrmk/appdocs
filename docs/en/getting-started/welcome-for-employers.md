Heurs gives employers one central workspace for employees, time registrations, leave and organization settings.

## The main areas

- **Employees:** invite team members and monitor their current status.
- **Time registrations:** review submitted weeks and follow up on missing entries.
- **Leave:** assess requests with visibility into team availability.
- **Settings:** define the workweek, staffing target, leave policy and notifications.

::: info Keep the employee experience simple
Employees register their hours and request leave. You configure the organization, monitor progress and process items that need approval.
:::

## Recommended setup order

1. Verify your organization details.
2. Configure the standard workweek and leave policy.
3. Invite employees.
4. Review the dashboard when the first registrations arrive.

## Daily workflow

Start with the employee overview, then check notifications, time registrations and leave requests. Items without an outstanding action do not require a decision.
