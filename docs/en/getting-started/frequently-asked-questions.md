## Why is an employee still marked as invited?

The employee has not completed account activation. Verify the email address and ask them to check spam. Create a new invitation when the previous link has expired.

## Why are hours shown as missing?

The selected week is incomplete or has not been submitted. Also check whether the employee workweek and approved leave are configured correctly.

## What happens after I approve a time registration?

The week is marked as processed and is included in the relevant organization overviews. Return the registration instead when a correction is required.

## Why does a leave request affect availability?

Heurs compares planned absence with the configured working days and staffing target. The signal helps you assess coverage before deciding.

## Can I change organization settings later?

Yes. Review the effective date and possible impact on future calculations before saving a policy change.

## I cannot find the answer

Contact [support@heurs.nl](mailto:support@heurs.nl) and include the page, period and employee concerned. Do not send passwords or verification codes.
