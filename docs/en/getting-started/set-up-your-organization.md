---

Correct organization settings ensure that time, leave and availability calculations are reliable from the start.

## Check the basics

Open **Settings** and verify the organization name and the details used throughout your workspace.

## Recommended order

1. Select the days that count as working days.
2. Set the target for team availability.
3. Review the leave policy and its effective date.
4. Choose the time reminders and email summaries your team needs.

::: tip Configure first, invite second
When you finish the organization settings before inviting employees, new team members immediately see the correct workweek and leave rules.
:::

## Verify the result

Open the employee and leave overviews. Confirm that working days, availability signals and calculated values look correct before communicating the policy to your team.
