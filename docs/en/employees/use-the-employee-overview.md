The employee overview is the central starting point for team management.

## Understand statuses

| Status | Meaning | Possible action |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Active | The profile has been activated. | Review time, leave or employee details. |
| Invited | The invitation has not been accepted yet. | Wait or verify the email address. |
| Expired | The activation link is no longer valid. | Create a new invitation. |

## Refine the list

Search by name or email. Use status and role filters to find open invitations or employees within a specific role.

## Open employee details

Select an employee to review or update profile details. Verify contract hours and the hourly rate carefully; registered hours above contract hours are shown as overtime.
