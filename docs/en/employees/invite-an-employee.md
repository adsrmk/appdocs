Add new team members from the employee overview.

## Before you start

Use the business email address the employee will use for Heurs. Check the spelling carefully because the invitation and future profile are linked to this address.

## Send an invitation

1. Open **Employees**.
2. Select **Invite employee**.
3. Enter the employee name and email address.
4. Create the invitation and verify its delivery status.

## After sending

The employee receives an activation email. Until activation, the employee appears with the status **Invited**. If the link expires, create a new invitation; the previous activation link will become invalid.

::: warning No email received?
Check the address and ask the employee to inspect their spam folder before creating a new invitation.
:::
