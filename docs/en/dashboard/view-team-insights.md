The team overview helps you monitor staffing and effort without opening every registration separately.

## Use the weekly view

Choose a week to compare team members within the same period. Look for missing hours, planned leave and unusual attendance values.

## Read the overview

- **Hours worked:** the registered total for the selected week.
- **Contract:** the agreed weekly hours and recorded hourly rate.
- **Last working day:** the most recent day with a time entry.
- **Status:** active, invited or expired.
- **Leave balance:** the current balance recorded for the employee.

## Search, filter and export

Search by name or email, filter by status or role and sort by hours worked. Open an employee to review profile details, contract hours and calculated overtime.
