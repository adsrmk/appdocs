Review the dates, requested hours, leave balance and impact on the team before approving or rejecting a request.

## Review a request

1. Open **Leave** and select a pending request.
2. Check the employee, dates and requested number of hours.
3. Review overlapping leave and expected team availability.
4. Approve or reject the request.
5. Add a clear explanation when a request is rejected.

## After your decision

Approved leave is included in availability and time overviews. Rejected leave does not change the balance. Recheck the team view when the request covers a busy period.
