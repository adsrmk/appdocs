---
title: Check team availability
description: Review planned absence and prevent understaffing.
outline: deep
---

Team availability combines working days and approved leave to show how much capacity remains.

## Choose the right period

Open the leave or team overview and select the period covered by the request. Compare planned absence across employees instead of reviewing a request in isolation.

## Interpret the signal

Use the target availability from your organization settings as a warning threshold. A low value does not automatically require rejection, but it signals that you should review coverage and priorities.

## Resolve conflicts

Discuss alternatives with the employee when too many team members are absent at the same time. Record the final decision in Heurs so balances and overviews stay consistent.
