title: Configure the leave policy
description: Manage accrual, balances and organization rules for leave.
outline: deep
---

A clear leave policy keeps employee balances and approval decisions consistent.

## Define the policy

Record how leave is granted or accrued, which effective date applies and how existing balances should be handled. Align the configuration with employment agreements and applicable rules.

## Verify employee balances

After saving, review a small number of employee profiles. Confirm that balances and future accrual match your intended policy before using them for decisions.

## Change an existing policy

Choose an appropriate effective date and document the reason for the change. Avoid silently rewriting historical decisions or balances.
