title: Configure time reminders and notifications
description: Choose when your team receives reminders and summaries.
outline: deep

Reminders help employees submit hours on time and help managers keep outstanding work visible.

## Choose useful reminders

Enable reminders that match the submission rhythm of your organization. A weekly reminder is usually clearer than several messages about the same period.

## Configure manager summaries

Use summaries to highlight missing registrations and requests waiting for approval. Keep the recipient list limited to people who can act on the information.

## Prevent notification fatigue

Review reminder settings after the first few weeks. Disable duplicates and adjust timing when messages arrive too early or after the team has already completed the task.
