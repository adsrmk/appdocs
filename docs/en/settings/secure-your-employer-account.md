Employer accounts can access sensitive workforce information. Use a unique password and enable two-step verification.

## Enable two-step verification

1. Open your account or security settings.
2. Start the two-step verification setup.
3. Scan the QR code with an authenticator app.
4. Enter the generated code to confirm setup.
5. Store recovery information in a secure location.

## Keep access secure

- Never share your password or verification codes.
- Remove access for people who no longer manage the organization.
- Review unexpected login or account-change emails immediately.
- Contact support when you lose access rather than creating a second account.
